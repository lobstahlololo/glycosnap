# glycothing

GlycoSnap is an AI-powered nutrition and health tracking app designed to help you manage your diet, track blood sugar trends, and manage diabetes. By using your smartphone camera or text descriptions, the app analyzes your food to provide instant nutritional breakdowns and glucose predictions.
